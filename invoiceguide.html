So what is an invoice, anyway?

An invoice is a bill that records the fact that you provided a product or service and someone owes you payment for it. Want to learn more about invoices before moving forward? Check out our blog post titled "What is An Invoice?".

Why do I need invoices?

If the product or service you’re selling isn’t paid in full up front, you need a formal way to request payment or to keep track of payments from customers. Invoices meet both these business needs.

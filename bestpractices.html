Best Practices


How do I make the invoicing process as easy and low-maintenance as possible?

Here are some tips:

Create invoices that have all the information your customer needs

This makes it easier for both you and your customer to refer back to the invoice in the future.including phone numbers and email addresses makes it easy to follow-up. You never know who might be processing your invoice - accounts payable might need a quick phone call or email to clear up any questions.

Number your invoices, download them and save them in a place that is easily accessible

This way you’ll always have a unique number associated with each one, and finding them later will be a snap if they are saved in one place.

Send your invoices on time and to the right person.

Do some research on payment terms that are effective in your field, and try out a few to see which ones work best.
